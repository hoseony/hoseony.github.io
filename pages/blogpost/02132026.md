# Missing Semester
For my first blog post, I thought it would be nice to share resources and software that I have found helpful in various ways.

## 1. Missing Semester
[https://missing.csail.mit.edu/](https://missing.csail.mit.edu/)

I haven't had a chance to take a close look at their new lectures yet. However, I have watched their 2020 lectures and found them very useful.

## 2. Logisim Evolution
[https://github.com/logisim-evolution/logisim-evolution](https://github.com/logisim-evolution/logisim-evolution)

If you are doing anything related to digital logic design, Logisim Evolution is well-made software for simulating your logic.

## 3. Obsidian
[https://obsidian.md/](https://obsidian.md/)

Obsidian is the best note-taking software that I have used so far. I use GitHub to sync my notes. You should give it a try.
