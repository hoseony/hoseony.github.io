# Missing Semester
As of my first blog post, I thought it would be nice to share resources and softwares that I found very helpful in various ways.

## 1. Missing Semester
[https://missing.csail.mit.edu/](https://missing.csail.mit.edu/)

I haven't had chance to really take a look at their new lectures. Though, I have watched their lectures from 2020 and found it very useful.

## 2. Logisim Evolution
[https://github.com/logisim-evolution/logisim-evolution](https://github.com/logisim-evolution/logisim-evolution)

If you are doing anything related to Digital Logic Design, Logisim Evolution is such a well made software to simulate your logic. 

## 3. Obsidian
[https://obsidian.md/](https://obsidian.md/)

Obsidian is the best note taking software that I have used so far. I use github to sync my notes. You should give a try.
